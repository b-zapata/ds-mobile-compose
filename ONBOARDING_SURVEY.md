Generic Onboarding Survey

1. Trait 1: "What is the #1 character trait of your 'Best Self'? (e.g., Disciplined, Brave)"
2. Trait 2: "What quality do you admire most in people you look up to? (e.g., Focus, Consistency)"
3. Trait 3: "What is one trait you are actively trying to develop this year? (e.g., Patience, Presence)."
4. Goal 1: "What is the most important academic or professional goal you’re working toward right now?"
5. Goal 2: "What is a personal passion or skill you want to improve (e.g., Guitar, Photography)?"
6. Goal 3: "What is a long-term dream you hope to achieve?"
7. Role 1: “What is a role you have where someone depends on you?” (e.g., mentor, older sibling, teammate, best friend)
8. Role 2: "How would you describe your primary role in the world right now? (e.g., student, creator, aspiring engineer)."
9. Role 3: " What is a role or identity you want to live up to?”
10. Automaticity: “How often do you open your most-used app without thinking about it?” (5-point Likert scale: Not often at all – Very often)
11. Utility: “After spending time scrolling, how do you usually feel?” (5-point Likert scale: Much worse – Much better).
12. Intention: “To what extent do you want to reduce the amount of time you spend scrolling?” (5-point Likert scale: Not at all – Very)
